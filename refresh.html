    DECONSTRUCTION RECONSTRUCTION: REFRESH

    2020

    2 Series Android application-based interactive art

    2 Android tablet devices

    -------------------------------------------

    Deconstruction Reconstruction was planned to be a series of android application which can be downloaded and collected through the internet. The aim is to give a chance to the viewer to interact and collect the work instead of just seeing it installed in a gallery. The artist purposely wants to be as close as possible with the viewer by taking aside the gap between her and viewers since an artwork mainly can only be seen in a gallery or an art space.

    2020 is a unique year that makes people countlessly hoping for the pandemic to be over. Factually, it has changed the many elements of life. Including behaviour and culture. People around the globe are forced to stay and work at home. Therefore, again, computers and mobile telephone appeared to be the main core of our everyday lives.

    In Refresh, the shape and colour through the code that works inside of it change its appearance into a presumable look of a geometrical starflower. The interactivity within the work represents the artist’s wishes to present novelty towards life throughout the global situation regarding the apocalypse that was arisen by the current pandemic.
