    24 GRIDS LIVING IN HARMONY OF DUST AND HEAT

    2014

    Multi-channel Video - Sync

    -------------------------

    For 24 years the artist was born and lived in this city found that Cikarang has gone through a phase that begins with the agrarian social system that evolved into modern society. Irregularities that occurred in the city of Cikarang has resulted in dirty looks and veil to the environment.

    Disorders appear in Cikarang, not just as a result of environmental contamination/pollution which is arising from the pollution of water, air, and soil as well as sound or noise, but also through the pressure arising from the economic, social and employment.

    This work is expected to be used as a picture of Cikarang through the pieces of recording that are presented in a subjectively perspective (personal). Through video channel amalgamation, creator donated most of the basic stories and insights that stem from the experience of living for 24 years in Cikarang.
