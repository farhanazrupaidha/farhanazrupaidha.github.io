    DECONSTRUCTION RECONSTRUCTION: BABEL

    2018

    6 Series Android application-based interactive art

    6 Android tablet devices

    -------------------------------------------

    Deconstruction Reconstruction was planned to be a series of android application which can be downloaded and collected through the internet. The aim is to give a chance to the viewer to interact and collect the work instead of just seeing it installed in a gallery. The artist purposely wants to be as close as possible with the viewer by taking aside the gap between her and viewers since an artwork mainly can only be seen in a gallery or an art space.

    Given the time on what is happening right now in Indonesia where the societies are experiencing the highly emotional tension of the political situation and several natural disasters that was occurred in some regions, it is also the time to deconstruct and reconstruct the many factors in life. Those factors are inevitably taking places somewhere on the internet with many false words and fake visuals scattered randomly.

    The algorithmic system made by code has given a new meaning to people which they can deconstruct and reconstruct the many shapes of matter proportion whether it is in visually or in sound. While on the other side the ‘if’ function is naturally controlled the result of the changing.

    With Babel, people could tap one coordinate on their electronic device to have a result of visual reconstruction. Furthermore, through old ancient writings, Babel or the Tower of Babel was known as an interpretation of myth about diversity and the vanity of its people. The pomposity that human has in this work entitled Babel cannot eliminate the control basis through the ‘if’ function just as a human cannot assert that his existence is the highest and is on top position of God.
